# Faster Maturity 2.0

**Faster Maturity** increases the maturation speed of characters to provide a more believable medieval progression. Characters reach the age of reason earlier, complete their education at a younger age, and gain access to military service and marriage according to historically inspired age limits. The mod also expands gameplay with additional fertility and childbirth mechanics.

## Main Features

* Faster and more realistic character maturation.
* Earlier education completion and military service.
* Includes the Impregnate Spouse system.
* Female fertility age increased from 40 to 45.
* Higher infant mortality and childbirth death mechanics.
* Supports both English and Spanish localization.

## Compatibility

* Compatible with the latest game version.
* Save game compatible.
* Can be used with most mods that do not modify the same maturation mechanics.
* Includes the AGOT compatibility patch by @NeosAlexandros.
